WHO WE ARE

ABOUT US

KHLG is a licensed corporation with a Certificate of Authorization to operate in Construction Management (CM), Civil Engineering, and Environmental Engineering firm under the Guam Board of Registration for PEALS. Founded in January 2019, KHLG is a locally owned, Guam-based Engineering firm providing technical, managerial, and construction-related services throughout Guam, Marianas and Micronesia region. The firm integrates global best practices with regional insight to deliver efficient, compliant, and sustainable project outcomes. KHLG found its success through its licensed and certified personnel with specialized and diverse experience for more than 40 years in the fields of engineering, project management, construction management, environmental services, and construction claims. KHLG’s experience expands from the public to private sector, which includes aviation, marine, building facilities, waterworks, and roadway-related projects. KHLG’s proximity to island communities of Guam, Marianas and Micronesia region allows us to provide responsive, regionally attuned, and culturally informed services, supporting the growth and infrastructure development of clients .

MISSION & VISION

To be the leading engineering partner in Guam, Marianas, and Micronesia, focusing on project and construction management, civil engineering, and environmental engineering and consulting services. Our goal is to be recognized for technical excellence, reliability, and innovative solutions, with an emphasis on accountability, diversity, teamwork, and excellence for our Clients. KHLG is committed to delivering high-quality, value-driven services with integrity, excellence, innovation, collaboration, and partnership. We create sustainable and resilient solutions that respond to evolving environmental and infrastructure challenges. We strive to strengthen communities, support responsible development, and foster long-term client relationships built on trust, performance, and shared success.

OUR LEADERSHIP

KHLG is comprised of a highly skilled and multidisciplinary team with extensive experience in construction management, civil engineering and environmental services. Our professionals collectively bring over 100 years of combined expertise in construction oversight, engineering support, environmental consulting, construction claims analysis, and technology-driven project solutions. This depth and diversity of experience enable us to deliver comprehensive, innovative, and results-driven infrastructure services across every phase of project delivery. Our leadership team is dedicated to driving excellence through strategic direction, collaborative engagement, and a commitment to continuous improvement. By fostering a culture grounded in accountability, innovation, and professional growth, KHLG ensures that every project is executed with precision, efficiency, and a strong focus on client success.

CORE VALUES

At KHLG, SAFETY is not a priority—it is a core value and a Zero-Tolerance standard embedded in everything we do. We are unwavering in our commitment to protecting our people, partners, clients, and the public through rigorous safety protocols, proactive risk management, and a culture of accountability at every level of our organization. Every project begins and ends with safety as the foundation.
