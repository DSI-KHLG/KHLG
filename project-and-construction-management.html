PROJECT AND CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT

KHLG provides comprehensive Project and Construction Management services focused on delivering projects safely, efficiently, and in full compliance with project requirements. Our approach integrates proven management practices, industry-standard inspections, and strict adherence to U.S. Regulatory Frameworks to ensure every phase of execution meets the highest standards of quality and accountability. We leverage advanced digital tools and integrated project control systems including real-time scheduling, cost tracking, and performance monitoring. Leveraging the latest technologies maintain clear alignment with project scope, schedule, and budget. Through proactive planning, continuous coordination, and data-driven decision-making, our team identifies risks early, implements effective mitigation strategies, and ensures seamless communication among all stakeholders. By combining our 100-year technical expertise, disciplined execution, and a commitment to transparency, KHLG consistently delivers projects that meet client expectations while maintaining safety, regulatory compliance, and long-term project success.

KEY SERVICES

Delivering Safe, Compliant, and Efficient Infrastructure Projects from Planning to Completion.

Schedule Management

Developing and managing project schedules using CPM analysis, baseline tracking, and Primavera P6 for progress and variance control.

Web-Based Construction Progress Tracking

Using Procore with certified experts to ensure efficient project control and collaboration.

Project Planning & Development

Comprehensive front-end planning that aligns design, risk, and execution for successful project delivery.

Local & Federal Agency Coordination

Regulatory compliance and coordination to ensure approvals and standrads are met.

Construction Inspection & Quality Monitoring

Thorough field inspections ensuring quality, proper installations, and adherence to standardized QA/QC procedures.

Photographic Documentation

Capturing real-time project progress with photos and live monitoring for clear milestone and site tracking.

Turnover, Acceptance, & Closeout Documentation

Managing project turnover documents, including as builts, test results, warranties, O&M manuals, and compliance records.

Punch List/Defects Management

Conducting field verification, identifying root causes, tracking resolutions, and documenting issue closure.

BUILT ON DISCIPLINE, DRIVEN BY RESULTS
