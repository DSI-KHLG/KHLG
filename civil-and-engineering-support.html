CIVIL & ENGINEERING SUPPORT

KHLG provides Civil and Engineering Support Services that contribute to the effective planning, coordination, and implementation of resilient and sustainable infrastructure projects. Our team works alongside project owners, designers, and contractors to strengthen project delivery through a combination of technical expertise, practical field insight, and a clear understanding of regulatory frameworks. We provide support on technical reviews and constructability analyses to ensure that designs are compliant, efficient, viable, and aligned with project objectives. By helping the key stakeholders in identifying potential conflicts, design gaps, and coordination challenges early in the process, we help reduce costly revisions, minimize delays, and improve overall project performance. Our team also supports regulatory coordination by facilitating communication with agencies, assisting with documentation, and ensuring that all our supports are in line with engineering aspects meet applicable codes, standards, and permitting requirements. During construction, KHLG offers engineering support that bridges the gap between design intent and field execution. We assist with design clarifications, review submittals, respond to technical inquiries, and provide recommendations that keep projects moving forward while maintaining quality and compliance. Through this integrated approach, we enhance collaboration across all project stakeholders, improve decision-making, and help deliver infrastructure that is durable, efficient, and responsive to both current needs and long-term demands.

CORE CAPABILITIES

Providing Expert Technical Support to Ensure Resilient, Constructible, and Code-Compliant Infrastructure.

SITE DEVELOPMENT & GRADING SUPPORT

Review of topographic data, cut-in-fill concepts, and site layout plans to support efficient land use and constructability.

UTILITY & INFRASTRUCTURE ENGINEERING SUPPORT

Technical review and coordination support for water, sewer, and stormwater systems to confirm capacity, reliability, and regulatory compliance.

DRAINAGE, STORMWATER, & EROSION CONTROL SUPPORT

Review of hydrologic analysis, Best Management Practices (BMP) selection, and erosion and sediment control measures to manage runoff and protect natural resources.

ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT MITIGATION SUPPORT

Support in evaluating engineering measures intended to minimize impacts to soil, water, and ecological resources during construction and operations.

REGULATORY PERMITTING

Independent review of plans, specifications, and cost estimate to support bidding, permitting, and field implementation.

ENGINEERING WITH PRECISION AND RESPONSIBILITY

KHLG’s Civil Engineering support approach is grounded in technical rigor, accountability, and a strong commitment to responsible project delivery. We help ensure that project documentation and construction activities are not only technically sound, but also environmentally responsible and fully aligned with applicable local, territorial, and U.S. Federal standards. By integrating quality assurance measures, thorough reviews, and constructability assessments early in the project lifecycle, we proactively identify and address potential issues before they escalate. Our team works closely with stakeholders to maintain consistency between design intent and field execution, minimizing discrepancies that can lead to rework or delays. Through this disciplined and detail-oriented approach, KHLG reduces project risk, enhances efficiency, and supports smoother project progression and ultimately safeguarding schedules, controlling costs, and delivering outcomes that meet both regulatory expectations and client objectives.
