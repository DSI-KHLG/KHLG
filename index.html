PROFESSIONAL MENTORSHIP PROGRAM

KHLG has its Professional Mentorship Program that formalized academic partnerships in collaboration with the University of Guam Triton Advising Center and endorsed by the UOG School of Engineering. This program not only offers invaluable hands-on experience to the students but also fosters a collaborative environment where seasoned professionals mentor and guide the next generation of construction managers and engineers.

Through the Professional Mentorship Program, KHLG is not only constructing infrastructure projects but also building a pathway for the growth and development of future leaders in the construction industry.
