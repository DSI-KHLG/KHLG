WHO WE ARE

ABOUT KHLG

KHLG is built on decades of combined expertise, deep regional knowledge, and a commitment to delivering thoughtful, high-quality solutions. Our team brings together practical field experience and technical depth to approach each project with clarity, precision, and a strong understanding of local conditions across Guam, the Marianas, and Micronesia. This foundation allows us to anticipate challenges, adapt to project-specific demands, and provide solutions that are both effective and contextually grounded. We approach every engagement with disciplined execution, integrity, and a focus on long-term value. By working closely with clients and stakeholders, we foster strong collaboration, maintain transparency, and ensure that project objectives are met with consistency and professionalism. Our commitment extends beyond delivery—we aim to build lasting partnerships by providing reliable guidance, maintaining high standards of quality, and supporting successful outcomes at every stage of the project lifecycle.

MISSION & VISION

To be the leading engineering partner in Guam, Marianas, and Micronesia, focusing on project and construction management, civil engineering, and environmental engineering and consulting services. Our goal is to be recognized for technical excellence, reliability, and innovative solutions, with an emphasis on accountability, diversity, teamwork, and excellence for our Clients. KHLG is committed to delivering high-quality, value-driven services with integrity, excellence, innovation, collaboration, and partnership. We create sustainable and resilient solutions that respond to evolving environmental and infrastructure challenges. We strive to strengthen communities, support responsible development, and foster long-term client relationships built on trust, performance, and shared success.

OUR LEADERSHIP

KHLG is comprised of a highly skilled and multidisciplinary team with extensive experience in environmental services, civil engineering, and construction management. Our professionals collectively bring over 100 years of combined expertise in engineering design, construction oversight, environmental consulting, construction claims analysis, and technology-driven project solutions. This depth and diversity of experience enable us to deliver comprehensive, innovative, and results-driven infrastructure services across every phase of project delivery. Our leadership team is dedicated to driving excellence through strategic direction, collaborative engagement, and a commitment to continuous improvement. By fostering a culture grounded in accountability, innovation, and professional growth, KHLG ensures that every project is executed with precision, efficiency, and a strong focus on client success.

CORE VALUES

At KHLG, SAFETY is not a priority—it is a core value and a Zero-Tolerance standard embedded in everything we do. We are unwavering in our commitment to protecting our people, partners, clients, and the public through rigorous safety protocols, proactive risk management, and a culture of accountability at every level of our organization. Every project begins and ends with safety as the foundation.
