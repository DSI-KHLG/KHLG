Scheduling & Project Controls

KHLG applies advanced scheduling and project control systems to maintain real-time visibility into project performance. Utilizing industry-leading tools such as Oracle Primavera P6, our specialists develop and manage Critical Path Method (CPM) schedules, monitor earned value metrics, and produce comprehensive progress and forecasting reports. All scheduling activities are conducted in alignment with contract requirements and industry best practices to support proactive schedule management and successful project delivery.
