KHLG & UNIVERSITY OF GUAM:

Academic Partnership & Professional Program

KHLG in collaboration with the University of Guam (UOG) Triton Advising Center and endorsed by the UOG School of Engineering has its Professional Mentorship Program that formalized academic partnerships. The KHLG team and its clients have a partnership with the University that will integrate UOG School of Engineering students into KHLG project team for the execution of project and construction management projects. This program offers invaluable hands-on experience to the students and fosters a collaborative environment where seasoned professionals mentor and guide the next generation of engineers.

Through this Program, KHLG is building a pathway for the growth and development of future leaders in the construction industry. The stakeholders will have direct access to recruit and hire locally educated engineers with experience in large-scale construction and working with the Government of Guam following their graduation from the university. This model ensures that operational and regulatory knowledge is transferred, while simultaneously expanding Guam’s pool of qualified engineering experts.
