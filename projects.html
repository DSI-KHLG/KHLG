GIAA Aircraft Rescue and Fire Fighting (ARFF) Facility Phase II

This project was performed in full compliance with GIAA's Airport Security Program, Security directives, Emergency Amendments, and FAA regulations.

The construction activities included but were not limited to the construction of a new, regulatory, compliant ARFF Facility to acctommodate: 1. The station’s apparatus bays; 2. Living and working quarters to accommodate the corresponding number of personnel to operate the ARFF apparatuses; 3. Ancillary facilities, and 4. A 20k gallon steel water tank.

Department of Public Works - Guam National Tennis Center (GNTC)

A USTA-designed facility featuring 11 full-sized and 4 mini-sized tennis courts.

In addition to the tennis courts, the Guam National Tennis Center, (GNTC) building, includes restrooms, with lockers and shower facilities, an office, covered common area spaces, storage rooms, and upper deck rooftop seating for 360◦ views of the facility.

ENVIRONMENTAL SERVICES

Navigating complex regulatory landscapes while protecting our region's unique ecosystems. We provide comprehensive environmental assessment, mitigation, and compliance strategies.

  • Quality Assurance MCBCB Environmental Support (Jan 2024 – Present)

    Conducts regulatory-compliant design reviews, site inspections, and technical documentation per the 2006 Guam/CNMI Stormwater Management Manual.

  • Life Support Area Environmental Design Support (May 2022)

    Revises erosion control plans, develops SWPPP, and redesigns post-construction BMPs and sediment basins.

  • Environmental Condition of Property Report - Guam Army National Guard (Sep 2022)

    Fieldwork and development of an ECP/PCA report in accordance with National Guard, Army, and ASTM standards.
