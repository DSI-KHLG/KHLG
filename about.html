ABOUT KHLG

KHLG is built on decades of combined expertise, regional knowledge, and a commitment to delivering thoughtful, high-quality solutions. Our team brings practical insight, technical depth, and disciplined execution to every project, supporting clients across Guam, the Marianas, and Micronesia with integrity and professionalism.

MISSION & VISION

To be the leading engineering partner in Guam, Marianas and Micronesia, focusing in project & construction management, civil engineering, and environmental engineering and consulting services. Our goal is to be recognized for technical excellence, reliability, and innovative solutions with an emphasis on accountability, diversity, teamwork, and excellence for our Clients. 
KHLG is committed to delivering high-quality, value-driven services with integrity, excellence, innovation, collaboration and partnership. 

OUR LEADERSHIP

KHLG is comprised of a highly skilled and multidisciplinary team with extensive experience in environmental services, civil engineering, and construction management. Our professionals collectively bring over 100 years of combined expertise in engineering design, construction oversight, environmental consulting, construction claims analysis, and technology-driven project solutions. This depth and diversity of experience enable us to deliver comprehensive, innovative, and results-driven infrastructure services across every phase of project delivery. 

CORE VALUES

At KHLG, SAFETY is not a priority—it is a core value and a zero-tolerance standard embedded in everything we do. We are unwavering in our commitment to protecting our people, partners, clients, and the public through rigorous safety protocols, proactive risk management, and a culture of accountability at every level of our organization. Every project begins and ends with safety as the foundation.
