Environmental Services

KHLG offers comprehensive Environmental Services that support clients in navigating regulatory requirements, managing environmental risks, and advancing sustainable project outcomes. Our team provides strategic advisory and program-level expertise in environmental planning, permitting, and compliance to ensure projects are delivered responsibly while meeting long-term regulatory and environmental stewardship objectives.

KEY SERVICES

Strategic Environmental Solutions for Responsible and Sustainable Project Delivery.

ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE AND PERMITTING

Identifying regulations, permit requirements, and compliance obligations across all project phases.

ENVIRONMENTAL MANAGEMENT PLANNING

Developing pollution prevention, waste management, spill response, and monitoring programs tailored to project needs.

REMEDIATION AND RESTORATION PLANNING*

Includes assessing environmental impacts, developing strategies to remove or contain hazards, ensuring regulatory compliance, and designing restoration plans.

Evaluating potential impacts and preparing proactive mitigation and monitoring plans.

EXPERT CONSULTING FOR COMPLIANCE AND EFFICIENCY
