PROJECT AND CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT

KHLG provides comprehensive Project and Construction Management services focused on delivering projects safely, efficiently, and in full compliance with project requirements. Our approach combines proven management practices, industry-standard inspection protocols, U.S. regulatory compliance frameworks, and integrated digital project controls to maintain alignment with project scope, schedule, and budget. From early planning and procurement through construction oversight and project closeout, KHLG ensures disciplined coordination, quality assurance, and transparent project execution that supports reliable and successful infrastructure delivery. 

KEY SERVICES

Delivering Safe, Compliant, and Efficient Infrastructure Projects from Planning to Completion. 

👷

Project Planning & Development

Comprehensive front-end planning that aligns design, risk, and execution for successful project delivery.

👷

Local & Federal Agency Coordination

Managing project turnover documents, including as-builts, test results, warranties, O&M manuals, and compliance records.

Construction Inspection & Quality Monitoring

Thorough field inspections ensuring quality, proper installations, and adherence to standardized QA/QC procedures..

Schedule Management

Turnover, Acceptance, & Closeout Documentation

Regulatory compliance and coordination to ensure approvals and standards are met.

Web-Based Construction Progress Tracking

Photographic Documentation

Capturing real-time project progress with photos and live monitoring for clear milestone and site tracking.
